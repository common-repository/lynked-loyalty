Lynked Loyalty's Woocommerce plugin lets businesses integrate our rewards system both online and in-store, offering a seamless and modern loyalty experience.

== Description ==

Lynked Loyalty is a state-of-the-art loyalty solution that offers a seamless and convenient way for customers to collect and redeem rewards. With our Woocommerce plugin, businesses can easily integrate our platform into their online and physical stores, providing customers with the flexibility to collect points through the Lynked Loyalty app on both iOS and Android devices.

Our loyalty technology is designed to drive customer engagement and increase revenue for businesses. By allowing customers to collect points online and in-store, businesses can bring customers back more frequently, driving repeat purchases. In addition, with delayed voucher activation and voucher expiry dates, businesses can increase revenue and drive urgency for customers to redeem their rewards.

Lynked Loyalty provides an omnichannel solution that caters to both online and physical businesses. With its seamless integration, businesses can offer a unified and consistent loyalty experience across all touchpoints, improving customer satisfaction and loyalty.


== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

1. Upload the plugin files to the /wp-content/plugins/lynked-loyalty directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the ‘Plugins’ screen in WordPress
3. Use the Lynked screen to configure the plugin

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What is Lynked Loyalty? =
Lynked Loyalty is a loyalty technology that enables customers to collect and redeem rewards both online and in-store. While customers don't need to use the app when shopping online, they can use the Lynked Loyalty app when shopping in-person at participating businesses. The app is available on both iOS and Android devices. With our Woocommerce plugin, businesses can easily integrate our platform into their online and physical stores.

= How does Lynked Loyalty work? =
Customers can collect points through the Lynked Loyalty app by making purchases at participating businesses. Points can be collected both online and in-person. Businesses can configure the rewards program to their preference, such as voucher activation delay and voucher expiry dates, to drive customer engagement and increase revenue.

= Who can use Lynked Loyalty? =
Lynked Loyalty is designed for both online and physical businesses. Businesses of all sizes can benefit from our platform by easily integrating it into their Woocommerce store.

= Is Lynked Loyalty available on iOS and Android? =
Yes, the Lynked Loyalty app is available on both iOS and Android devices.

= How does Lynked Loyalty help businesses increase revenue? =
Lynked Loyalty helps businesses increase revenue by allowing them to configure the rewards program to their preference, such as voucher activation delay and voucher expiry dates. This creates a sense of urgency for customers to redeem their rewards, driving repeat purchases and increasing revenue.

= How does Lynked Loyalty improve customer engagement? =
Lynked Loyalty improves customer engagement by offering a convenient and seamless way for customers to collect and redeem rewards. The omnichannel solution caters to both online and physical businesses, providing a unified and consistent loyalty experience across all touchpoints. This improves customer satisfaction and loyalty, driving customer engagement.
